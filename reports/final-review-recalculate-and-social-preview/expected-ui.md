# Expected UI

`/admin/review` now has three separate zones:

1. Summary cards with production decision counts.
2. `Пересчёт предложений` panel with preview, safety badges, explicit confirmation, last result, and rollback.
3. Existing workspace publication panel with undo and safe publish controls.

Filters include:

- AUTO_READY;
- GROUP_REVIEW;
- MANUAL_REVIEW;
- BLOCKED_CONFLICT;
- DO_NOT_PUBLISH;
- Прочие товары;
- prepared/excluded and legacy confidence filters.

The safe publish button remains separate from recalculation.
